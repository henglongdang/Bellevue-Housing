Public Testimony Guide Placeholder
===================================

2. Register for public comment if required.
3. Prepare remarks that fit within a 3-minute speaking window.
4. State the request clearly: review how the flat 0.50% local REET affects affordability while preserving infrastructure funding.

Sample 3-minute script:

Dear Mayor and Council,

Thank you for hearing public comment. I ask the Council to review Bellevue's flat local REET structure and study whether a flexible or graduated local approach could protect middle-income buyers while maintaining capital-project revenue.

The concern is not whether infrastructure matters. It does. The concern is whether the same transaction charge behaves differently in a cooling housing market, when sellers may raise list prices to preserve net proceeds or withdraw listings instead of accepting lower offers.

Please direct staff to evaluate affordability impacts, revenue stability, and possible safeguards for parks, roads, transit-supportive improvements, and public safety facilities.

Thank you.
